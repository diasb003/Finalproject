What are we?

The Toronto Raptors, are a Canadian professional basketball team based in Toronto that plays in the Eastern Conference of the National Basketball Association (NBA). The Raptors have won one conference title and one NBA championship (both 2019). The Toronto raptors are the only team in the NBA located outside the USA. In recent years the raptors have became a much stonger champoionship team, to read more about this look at the Our sucess page. Before the sucess there was a lot of failures, year after year there were bigger stronger teams, to read more about the rich history of the raptors read the Past failures page. After doing it all becoming NBA champions the team lost many of their top players, and fell back down to one of the lowest ranked teams whinin a few seasons. But we are starting to rebuild, with lots of young talent and some of the best prospects in the league the future is looking bright for the raptors.
