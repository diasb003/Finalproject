The sucess of 2019

What happened in 2019

The 2018-19 Raptors were the first team of Raptors to ever make it to the NBA finals, and not only make it, win it. This was a very special and iconic year for Toronto and the NBA as a whole. This is the first time Toronto has won the NBA championship, and the first time a team outside of the USA has won the NBA title. It's very special that Canada finally brought home a champtionship after 72 years of the NBA being around. Basketball was invented by a Canadian after all, and 72 long years later the highest prestiege of honour when it comes to basketball has finally come home. Back to what happened in 2019, the raptors won due to many reasons, it wasn't a single element that lead to the win. There were lots of trades, and tough decesions that had to be made, we really put it all on the line to win. Masai Ujiri, the president of the raptors is the one to be thanked for this, he made all the hard decesions, and he had his mind fixated on winning the championship, and he made a lot of hard decesions in order to make that dream come true.
